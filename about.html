About Me

My name is Erica Gee, and I am 24 years old. I have been studying at Eastern Washignton Univeristy for the past two and a half years and will be graduating with a Bachelors in Marketing with a minor in Visual Communication Design (graphic design) in June of 2016. I've dabbled in many different majors, as I could not pick one early on in my college career. I've had experience with accounting, general business, graphic design, fashion design, and advanced math.

The ideal career for me would involve designing advertisements like I do in my current job as the grahpic designer for the advertising department of the schools newspaper: the Easterner. I've designed over 30 advertisements for various business in the Cheney/Spokane area for the newspaper, a few of which I have included in my portfolio.

I am an avid reader and love anything and everything related to the fantasy genre. I also enjoy science fiction, cheesy romance, anime, graphic novels, and Shakespeare. My all time favorite brand, franchise, book, and movie is Alice in Wonderland. I have a tendency to incorporate Alice in Wonderland themes into my personal work whenever possible.
